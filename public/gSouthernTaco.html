Southwestern Taco Salad

Learn how to make Southwestern Taco Salad

Southwestern Taco Salad Recipe by Tasty Team

Ingredients

Preparation

  1. To make the avocado cilantro lime salad dressing, blend or process all of the dressing ingredients (avocado, cilantro, lime juice, greek yogurt, water, salt, garlic, and cumin) until smooth. Remove from the equation.
  2. In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Stir in the diced chicken breast and taco seasoning until all of the pieces are evenly coated. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  3. Start by putting a bed of chopped romaine lettuce on the bottom of your salad. Then, either arrange the toppings in rows or blend everything together (chicken, cheddar jack cheese, black beans, avocado, corn, cherry tomatoes, and tortilla strips).
  4. Top off with the avocado salad dressing and cilantro then serve immediately.
  5. Enjoy!
